    The Spark of One Changed Life

    Imagine a student we'll call Favour. She's brilliant, but overlooked. She helps support her family and her schoolwork suffers, leaving her to believe that her dreams are out of reach. She sees others cheating and wonders if it's the only way to pass.

    Then our team arrives. It's not just another assembly. It's a message of worth. A mentor speaks with her, not *at* her. She hears a message of hope rooted in Christ's love—a message that tells her she is seen, valued, and created for a purpose. She receives a scholarship pack with the textbooks she couldn't afford.

    A single decision. She chooses to study. She chooses integrity. She chooses to believe in herself and her future. That is the impact. It's not just one life changed; it's a family's future, a community's leader, and a nation's hope, all set in motion by a single encounter.
